Become a Gitcoin Ambassador

Excited about open source? Want to help build the future of work?

The Gitcoin Ambassador team is a special group of community members that works on internal projects from Gitcoin Core. With a focus on community building, engagement, and growth, Gitcoin Ambassadors help shape the future of Gitcoin and work hand in hand with internal team members.

Why?

  • Grow Open Source Software
  • Work alongside our world-class team
  • Help organize Gitcoin events like Hacktoberfest and more
  • Stay up to date with the latest in blockchain and open source
  • Learn what it takes to build a community

Benefits

  • Copious amounts of Gitcoin Swag
  • Sponsorship for hosting local meetups
  • Access to mentorship from Gitcoin Core team members
  • A community ambassador badge on Gitcoin
  • (if it goes well) A grant to support you long term.

Responsibilites

  • Engaging in Gitcoin community channels (Slack, Twitter)
  • Helping with Issue Explorer Check Inâ€™s
  • Hosting local, in person meetups

Requirements

  • Passion for blockchain and open source
  • Knowledge of the Gitcoin Platform
  • Commitment to helping others
  • Esteemed English / written communication ability
  • 2-5 hours a week
